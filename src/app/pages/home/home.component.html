about us


The sole purpose of The Wedding Expo  is to showcase Africa’s finest wedding suppliers to thousands of planning couples across 4 expos every year.

Together with Wedding Inspirations Magazine, we are part of an organization, The Wedding Group. This gives rise to the most uniquely positioned platform in Africa that delivers significant value to all participants in the wedding industry in Africa.

Passionate about local businesses and providing the platform to grow them,  we believe in extending our value chain so they too become a beneficiary of our networks.

As wedding industry front runners, with 17 years of experience, The Wedding Expo is still going strong! Consistently striving for more, we are comitted to ensuring our events, which are the biggest in SA, are also the best!

Our team is made up of a group of diverse, power-house women who are as passionate about the wedding industry as any newly engaged couple!
